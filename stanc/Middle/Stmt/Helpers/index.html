val lvalue_base_reference : 'e Pattern.lvalue -> 'e Pattern.lvalue

Reduce an lvalue down to its "base reference", which is a variable with maximum tuple indices after it. For example:

x[1,2][3] -> x
x.1[1,2].2[3].3 -> x.1
x.1.2[1,2][3].3 -> x.1.2
